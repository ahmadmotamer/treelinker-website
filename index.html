Everything you need for your family archive

Six powerful features, beautifully organized in one app.

Family Tree Builder

Visualize your family relationships with any connection type. See how everyone is connected at a glance — parents, siblings, cousins, and more.

Memory Archive

Attach photos, notes, dates, and locations to the people in your tree. Nothing gets forgotten — your family's moments live alongside the people they belong to.

Family Groups

Invite relatives to shared spaces. Post memories, plan events, and stay connected — each group is private and invitation-only.

Private by Design

Your data lives on your device first. Local-first architecture means you're in control. Cloud sync is opt-in — not required to use the app.

Contact Integration

Link your phone's address book to your family tree. Recognize who's who without re-entering names and numbers you already have.

Smart Reminders

Never miss a birthday, anniversary, or family occasion. TreeLinker surfaces upcoming events for the people in your tree so you can reach out in time.

Up and running in minutes

Three steps to your living family archive.

1

Create your profile

Sign up with your name and email. TreeLinker creates your personal starting point in the family tree — you.

2

Add your family

Import from your contacts or add family members manually. Draw the relationships between them — parent, sibling, spouse, and more.

3

Capture & share

Add memories, photos, and notes. Invite relatives to a shared group and share the moments that matter to your whole family.

Built to protect your family's most personal moments

TreeLinker was designed with a local-first architecture. Your family's data lives on your device — the cloud is always opt-in, never required.

  • Your family's photos and notes never leave your device without your consent
  • Remote cloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time
  • We never sell or share your personal information with advertisers
  • Full account deletion removes everything — on your device and from our servers
