1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TreeLinker ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the TreeLinker mobile application. Where required by law, we will ask for your consent before accessing information such as your contacts or sending notifications.

This policy applies to the TreeLinker mobile application only — not to third-party services linked from within the app.

2. Our Core Philosophy: Local-First & User-Owned

Your family's data lives primarily on your device. Remote sync is an optional feature, not a requirement. You can use TreeLinker fully offline.

TreeLinker is built with a local-first architecture:

  • On-device storage is primary. Your family tree, contacts, memories, and photos are stored in a local database on your device. Every read operation hits local storage first.
  • Remote sync is optional. When enabled, your relationship graph is mirrored to our cloud database to support access from multiple devices. You can disable this in Settings at any time.
  • Your private data stays private. Personal notes, tags, and memories remain on your device unless you explicitly share them via a group invitation.
  • You own your data. You can export or delete your data at any time. Account deletion permanently erases all of your information from both local storage and our remote servers.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features.

Account & Profile Information
When you register, we collect your first name, last name, email address, phone number, and country code. Optionally, you may add a biography, location, and profile photo. Your password is hashed using the bcrypt algorithm before storage — we never retain your password in readable form.

Device Contacts
With your permission, TreeLinker securely processes your device contacts to help identify people you already know who use the app. Only normalized phone numbers are transmitted to our cloud database for matching. Contact names, notes, addresses, email addresses, and other contact information remain on your device and are never uploaded for this purpose.

Relationship Graph & Memories
We store the family connections you create, their relationship types, and the structure of your tree. We also store memories you associate with contacts or groups: text content, key dates, locations, and photos.

Photos
Photos are stored securely on your device. If cloud synchronization is enabled, photos are securely transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in our cloud database to keep your memories available across your devices.

Family Member Information
TreeLinker allows you to create family profiles for relatives and other individuals. You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to store and share information about these individuals. Information about family members is only shared when you explicitly choose to invite other users or share a group.

Push Notification Tokens
To send you real-time alerts about group invitations and shared memories, we collect your device's Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device's system settings or in TreeLinker's Settings screen.

Notification Preferences
We store your per-category notification preferences so your choices persist across sessions and devices.

Technical & Session Data
When you log in, we store a session identifier and last-activity timestamp in the secure keychain on your device. Sessions expire automatically after 30 days of inactivity.

4. App Permissions

TreeLinker requests access only to the permissions necessary to provide its features. You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve TreeLinker:

  • To create and maintain your account and authenticate your sessions.
  • To build, display, and navigate your family relationship graph.
  • To synchronise your data across your devices when remote sync is enabled.
  • To identify which of your contacts are registered TreeLinker users.
  • To send push notifications about group invitations and new memories, when enabled.
  • To allow you to share memories and group memberships with users you explicitly invite.
  • To support password recovery when you request a reset code.
  • To delete all of your data when you choose to close your account.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

With Other Users — Only When You Choose
When you invite someone to a group, your display name is shared with them via push notification. Memories added to a shared group become visible to other members of that group. You control all invitations and group membership at all times.

Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TreeLinker, our users, or the public.

Business Transfers
In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

8. Children's Privacy

TreeLinker is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us at ahmedmosttamer@gmail.com.

If we learn we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you are located in the European Economic Area, the age threshold may be 16 in some member states.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service:

  • Local data — retained on your device until you delete the app, reset your device, or delete your account.
  • Remote graph data — retained while your account is active. Permanently deleted when you delete your account.
  • Password reset codes — automatically deleted after use, or after 24 hours if unused.
  • Push tokens — removed from our systems when you log out or delete your account.
  • Group invitation records — retained for as long as the associated group exists.

If cloud synchronization is enabled, encrypted backup copies of synchronized data may temporarily exist as part of our disaster recovery procedures. These backups are retained only as long as necessary to ensure service reliability and are automatically removed according to our retention policies.

You can initiate complete deletion of all your data at any time through Settings → Delete Account in the app.

11. Security and Protection

We implement the following protections to keep your data secure:

  • Passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt before storage. We never hold your password in readable form.
  • All communication with cloud services is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Session tokens are securely stored using the platform's secure storage mechanisms (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Access to cloud infrastructure is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Password reset codes automatically expire after 24 hours and become invalid after repeated failed attempts.
  • Sessions expire automatically after 30 days of inactivity.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at ahmedmosttamer@gmail.com.

12. Analytics and Cookies

TreeLinker does not use cookies. As a native mobile application, it does not operate in a browser context and does not set or read browser cookies.

We do not include third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or Amplitude) in the app. We do not track your in-app behaviour for advertising or analytics purposes.

Firebase Cloud Messaging is used exclusively to deliver push notifications. We do not use Firebase Analytics or any other Firebase analytics product.

Your continued use of TreeLinker after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
